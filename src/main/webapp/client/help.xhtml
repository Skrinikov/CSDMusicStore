Register

Why should I register?

Registering gives you full access to all our services. If you want to buy music or leave reviews, you will have to be registered and loged on.

How do I register?

To register you will have to go to the page and fill out the form. Once the form is filled with the appropriate data, click the register button.
Albums

What can I find on the albums page?

The album page shows extensive information about a particular album. Some of the things you might see on the page are:
  • General info about the album.
  • Tracks of that album.
  • Reviews for each track in the album. If you are logged in, you will be able to leave a review on this page.
  • Prices of the album or a single track from this album.
  • Similar albums from different artists.

How do I buy the album?

To buy the album, locate the green button under the album's name. It will then add it to the shopping cart.
